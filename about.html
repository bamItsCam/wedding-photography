What We Do
Let's make the wedding photography business better

We believe we can improve the wedding experience of couples and photographers alike

Our Mission

We believe that a photographer's skill should be what matters, not the size of his or her network.

We believe that couples shouldn't feel the need to overpay for a photographer simply because they want a name that they can trust.

We believe there is untapped skill in the wedding photography world and a high demand for it: we aim to bring the two together.

How We Do It

Essentially, we pair wedding photographers we know and trust with couples looking for a photographer for their special day. Those applying to be a WU approved photographer first go through a rigorous application and screening process, including portfolio reviews and an in-person interview process. From there, we add a select few to our network, and begin matching couples with photographers based on availability, pricing, and experience.
